智能体人格动态交互框架

应用心理学（CSSCI） · 学生一作

摘要：人工智能的发展为人智交互带来了新的挑战，亟需构建更自然、可持续的交互方式。本文分析了智能时代人格化交互面临的三方面挑战，指出人智交互应以趋近人类交互为目标。在梳理现有人格心理学研究的基础上，本文提出智能体人格动态交互框架，并论证该框架在提升交互效果和解决智能发展挑战的潜力。通过分析该框架与传统任务导向人机交互范式之间的关系，为面向下一代人格化智能体的理论研究与应用提供新的方向。

Optimizing Design Processes through Data Intelligence under Resource Constraints

DRS 2026 Conference Proceedings · 第一作者

摘要：Enterprises operating under resource constraints often struggle to maintain efficient and innovative design practices. Data intelligence (DI) offers new opportunities to overcome design challenges and process optimization. However, research on how DI is practically integrated into real-world design workflows under such constraints remains limited. To address this gap, qualitative interviews were conducted with 21 industry designers from 14 companies. Using grounded theory, the interview data were coded to examine how DI supports the optimization of existing design processes and to identify its key intervention points and values. The study further investigates how DI interacts with design practices from multiple perspectives, including project type, organizational mode, and design strategy. By outlining a fifteen-dimensional blueprint of DI system functionalities, it provides guidance for future system development and proposes a framework for DI-driven design collaboration.
